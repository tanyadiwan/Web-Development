OUR MISSION

This medicine donator project is to prepare a portal for the collection of unused medicines for further utilization by a needy people.

The Website will aloow users to donate the unused medicine to NGO. Then NGO can help needy people.

PROBLEMS

Why we came up with this idea?

Leftover Medicines

India generates around three million tonnes of medical wastes every year and the amount is expected to grow at 8 % annually.

Poor people

A major population of our country lives in slums in unhygienic conditions, which becomes the foremost cause of ailments among the poor. These people do not have the resources to seek appropriate and affordable treatments.

Our step towards Society

In order to help them lead a healthy life, Medicine donation programs help the poor people, where they can be provided free medicines. And also helps prevention of wastage of medicines and reduces the problems which are caused due to unusual disposal and burning of leftover medicines
